# Erdős #686, k=5: exact Mordell-Weil packet audit

Date: 2026-07-16

Status: fourteen packets, both infinity vectors, and the combined
lattice-coset count are exact and reproducible. Rational-point completeness
is not proved.

## Fixed basis and known points

All packet coordinates use the ordered reduced-model Magma basis

```text
G1=(x^2-1/4,        3/2*x-3,       2)
G2=(x^2+x,         -6*x-3,         2)
G3=(x^2-1,         -6*x-3,         2)
G4=(x+1,           -6*x^3-9,       2)
G5=(x^2+1/2*x-1/2, -1/2*x+5/2,    2).
```

The 34 supplied affine vectors all satisfy the original genus-two equation
and survive all fourteen packets. The five designated affine vectors have
coordinate determinant `1`.

Exact Magma inversion gives the two missing projective vectors:

```text
original (1: 3:0), reduced (1: 6:0): [-2,0,0,0,0]
original (1:-3:0), reduced (1:-6:0): [0,1,0,1,0].
```

Both survive every packet. The final known projective set has 36 distinct
lattice vectors. After packets `7` and `11`, all 36 already lie in distinct
combined congruence classes.

## Phase-gate decision

The packet set is highly selective but still too broad for direct coset
enumeration. An invariant-only Magma scout was run at 18 candidate primes
from `61` through `149`. It computed the finite-group invariants and five
basis reductions, while the local exact code computed
`|phi_p(L_current)|`. Prime `139` is bad reduction and was discarded.

The strongest requests by genuinely absent invariant factor are:

| request rank | packet prime | invariants | exact relative gain | overlap | absent factor |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 1 | 107 | `[11717]` | 11717 | 1 | 11717 |
| 2 | 137 | `[20035]` | 4007 | 5 | 4007 |
| 3 | 101 | `[2,5998]` | 2999 | 4 | 2999 |
| 4 | 113 | `[13415]` | 2683 | 5 | 2683 |
| 5 | 127 | `[18770]` | 1877 | 10 | 1877 |

Thus the next full curve-image packet, if one is requested, should be
`p=107`. Its reduction image is cyclic of prime order `11717`, the current
lattice maps onto all of it, and none of that factor is present in the
fourteen-packet combined index. This recommendation is not based on taking
the largest available rational prime: for example `p=149` has total relative
gain `24991=67*373`, but only `373` is a new factor.
